### Field-Specific Adaptations

#### Life Sciences Tone
- **Narrative Style**: Clinical but accessible
- **Emphasis**: Patient impact and therapeutic potential
- **Examples**: "For patients with X condition..." "This could translate to..."
- **Expert Sources**: Clinicians, translational researchers

#### AI & Computing Tone
- **Narrative Style**: Technical but exciting
- **Emphasis**: Performance breakthroughs and applications
- **Examples**: "The model achieved..." "This enables..."
- **Expert Sources**: AI researchers, industry practitioners

#### Humanities Tone
- **Narrative Style**: Contextual and interpretive
- **Emphasis**: Cultural significance and historical context
- **Examples**: "This challenges our understanding of..." "From a cultural perspective..."
- **Expert Sources**: Cultural critics, historians, theorists

## Content Quality Templates

### Summary Writing Guidelines

#### Research Summary Template
```markdown
{{Lead finding}} from {{institution}} demonstrates {{method}} to solve {{problem}}.

The study, published in {{journal}}, used {{sample_size}} {{subjects}} to test {{hypothesis}}. Researchers found that {{key_result_1}} and {{key_result_2}}.

{{Methodology_details}} revealed {{surprising_finding}}. The team also discovered {{additional_insight}}.

This research represents a {{significance_level}} advance in {{field}} because {{implication_1}} and {{implication_2}}. Future applications could include {{future_use_1}} and {{future_use_2}}.
```

#### "Why This Matters" Template
```markdown
This matters because {{immediate_impact}}. For {{specific_group}}, this could mean {{direct_benefit}}. From a broader perspective, this {{paradigm_shift_or_opportunity}}. The approach also opens up possibilities for {{related_applications}}.
```

### Quality Checklist

#### Content Accuracy
- [ ] All facts, figures, and statistics verified
- [ ] Author names, institutions, and publication details correct
- [ ] Technical terminology used appropriately
- [ ] Claims supported by cited research
- [ ] Limitations and uncertainties acknowledged

#### Editorial Standards
- [ ] Content matches field definition and user interests
- [ ] Reading time estimates are accurate (¬±2 minutes)
- [ ] Links lead to authoritative sources
- [ ] No sensationalism or hype beyond research findings
- [ ] Multiple perspectives considered when relevant

#### Format Compliance
- [ ] Template structure followed correctly
- [ ] Brand voice and tone consistent
- [ ] Accessibility standards met (alt text, contrast)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design elements
- [ ] Call-to-action elements included

# Bite Size Academic - Personal Intelligence (PAI) Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Core Mission
*"Delivering academic excellence in bite-sized portions, empowering researchers to stay current without the overwhelm."*

### Brand Personality

#### Primary Attributes
- **Intellectual**: Respects the intelligence and expertise of our audience
- **Approachable**: Makes complex research accessible without oversimplifying
- **Efficient**: Values our users' time and attention
- **Curious**: Embodies the spirit of academic inquiry and discovery
- **Elegant**: Delivers content with sophistication and polish

#### Voice Characteristics
- **Knowledgeable but not condescending**: We're fellow academics, not lecturers
- **Concise but comprehensive**: Every word serves a purpose
- **Enthusiastic but not hype**: Genuine excitement about research breakthroughs
- **Precise but not pedantic**: Technical accuracy without unnecessary jargon
- **Forward-looking**: Connect research to future possibilities

### Brand Values

#### 1. Intellectual Rigor
- **Accuracy**: All facts, figures, and claims are verified
- **Context**: Research is presented with proper background and significance
- **Attribution**: All sources are properly credited
- **Balance**: Multiple perspectives are considered when appropriate

#### 2. User-Centered Design
- **Time Respect**: Content is optimized for busy schedules
- **Cognitive Load**: Information is presented in digestible chunks
- **Accessibility**: Content is available in multiple formats (text, audio)
- **Relevance**: Content is filtered for specific academic interests

#### 3. Academic Excellence
- **Quality over Quantity**: Fewer, more important research highlights
- **Interdisciplinary Connections**: Cross-field insights are highlighted
- **Emerging Trends**: Early identification of important developments
- **Diverse Voices**: Research from various institutions and perspectives

#### 4. Innovation in Communication
- **Modern Format**: Leveraging technology to enhance accessibility
- **Narrative Approach**: Stories that make research compelling
- **Visual Clarity**: Clean, scannable design
- **Multi-Modal**: Text, audio, and visual elements when appropriate

- **Font**: Georgia, serif (for quotes and special sections)

- **Primary Logo**: Text-based with subtle academic icon
- **Minimum Size**: 120px width for digital, 0.5" for print

## Content Standards

### Editorial Principles

#### Content Selection Criteria
1. **Significance**: Research with meaningful impact on the field
2. **Novelty**: New findings that advance understanding
3. **Quality**: Methodologically sound and peer-reviewed
4. **Accessibility**: Research that can be understood by educated non-specialists
5. **Relevance**: Timely and applicable to current academic discourse

#### Quality Assurance Process
1. **Source Verification**: All sources checked for credibility and accuracy
2. **Expert Review**: Content reviewed by subject matter experts when possible
3. **Fact-Checking**: All claims, statistics, and citations verified
4. **Bias Assessment**: Multiple perspectives considered
5. **Clarity Review**: Content edited for maximum comprehension

#### Content Tiers
- **Tier 1**: Breakthrough research with field-wide implications
- **Tier 2**: Significant advances within subfields
- **Tier 3**: Important methodological innovations
- **Tier 4**: Notable reviews and meta-analyses

### Writing Guidelines

#### Tone and Voice
- **Authoritative but Approachable**: Speak as knowledgeable colleagues
- **Concise but Complete**: Maximum information with minimum words
- **Precise but Accessible**: Technical accuracy with clear explanations
- **Engaging but Serious**: Professional enthusiasm without hype

#### Structure Guidelines
- **Inverted Pyramid**: Most important information first
- **One Main Idea**: Each item focuses on a single key finding
- **Context First**: Why this matters before technical details
- **Clear Signposting**: Headings and subheadings guide readers

#### Language Standards
- **Active Voice**: Use active constructions for clarity
- **Simple Sentences**: Average 15-20 words per sentence
- **Technical Terms**: Define or explain when necessary
- **Concrete Examples**: Use specific instances rather than abstractions

### Audio Content Standards

#### Podcast Characteristics
- **Duration**: 8-15 minutes per episode
- **Pacing**: 150 words per minute for optimal comprehension
- **Voice Quality**: Professional narration with clear articulation
- **Background**: Subtle ambient sounds for engagement

#### Audio Structure
1. **Introduction** (60-90 seconds): Episode overview and key themes
2. **Segment 1** (3-5 minutes): First research item with context
3. **Transition** (15-30 seconds): Bridge between topics
4. **Segment 2** (3-5 minutes): Second research item
5. **Conclusion** (60-90 seconds): Summary and future outlook

#### Audio Quality Standards
- **Technical Quality**: Clear audio without background noise
- **Consistency**: Same voice and style across episodes
- **Accessibility**: Full transcripts provided
- **Engagement**: Professional narration with appropriate tone

## User Experience Standards

### Interaction Design

#### User Interface Principles
- **Clarity**: Every element has a clear purpose
- **Efficiency**: Minimal steps to achieve goals
- **Consistency**: Predictable patterns across the platform
- **Feedback**: Clear confirmation of user actions

#### Accessibility Standards
- **WCAG 2.1 AA**: Full compliance for all content
- **Screen Readers**: Optimized for assistive technologies
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Complete keyboard accessibility
- **Color Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 for text, 3:1 for large text

#### Performance Standards
- **Load Time**: <2 seconds for all pages
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design for all screen sizes
- **Offline Capability**: Key content available offline
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Core functionality works everywhere

### Communication Standards

#### Email Communications
- **Subject Lines**: Clear, informative, consistent branding
- **Preview Text**: Compelling summary within 150 characters
- **Personalization**: User name and field preferences
- **Design**: Responsive HTML with plain text fallback

#### Support Communications
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours for all inquiries
- **Tone**: Helpful, professional, solution-oriented
- **Resolution**: Clear next steps and timelines
- **Follow-up**: Confirmation when issues are resolved

## Editorial Workflow

### Content Creation Process
1. **Source Discovery**: Automated and manual identification of relevant research
2. **Initial Screening**: Relevance and quality assessment
3. **Expert Review**: Subject matter validation when possible
4. **Content Creation**: Writing and editing according to brand standards
5. **Quality Assurance**: Final review for accuracy and clarity
6. **Format Adaptation**: Content optimized for email, web, and audio
7. **Distribution**: Delivery through appropriate channels

### Editorial Calendar
- **Monday**: Source discovery and initial screening
- **Tuesday**: Expert review and content creation
- **Wednesday**: Editing and quality assurance
- **Thursday**: Format adaptation and final review
- **Friday**: Distribution and audience feedback collection
- **Weekend**: Performance analysis and improvement planning

### Continuous Improvement
- **Audience Feedback**: Regular collection and analysis of user responses
- **Performance Metrics**: Open rates, click-through rates, engagement analysis
- **Content Review**: Monthly assessment of relevance and quality
- **Process Optimization**: Regular refinement of editorial workflows

## Brand Compliance

### Do's
- ✅ Maintain intellectual rigor in all content
- ✅ Respect the time and intelligence of our audience
- ✅ Provide proper attribution and context
- ✅ Use clear, accessible language without oversimplifying
- ✅ Maintain consistency across all touchpoints
- ✅ Prioritize accuracy over speed
- ✅ Consider multiple perspectives when appropriate

### Don'ts
- ❌ Oversimplify complex research
- ❌ Use hype or sensationalism
- ❌ Make claims beyond what the research supports
- ❌ Ignore methodological limitations
- ❌ Favor one institution or research group
- ❌ Compromise on quality for quantity
- ❌ Use jargon without explanation

This comprehensive brand guide ensures that every interaction with Bite Size Academic reflects our commitment to intellectual excellence, user-centered design, and innovative academic communication.
